The Vision of VERDΛ

Verda is planning on running a merchandise campaign where we will be giving away 20% of all revenue and up to half of our revenue on certain items to medical cannabis research. Together, we can end the stigma, we must break the norm.
Our goal is to change the way people view cannabis therefore ending the stigma associated with cannabis. We are advocates for extensive research for medical purposes as the prohibition has really limited the amount of current research done. We see Verda as one of the top donors for medical research through our merchandise campaign and contributing to researching hemp and all its potential uses
